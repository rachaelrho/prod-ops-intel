# Return & Refund Policy
**Internal Operations Guide**
*Last Updated: April 2026*

## Overview
This document outlines our return and refund procedures for operational decision-making. All returns must balance customer satisfaction with cost management and fraud prevention.

## Standard Return Window

**General Policy:**
- 30 days from delivery date for unused, unopened products
- Original packaging not required, but product must be in resalable condition
- Proof of purchase required (order number or email)

**Holiday Extension Period (November 15 - January 15):**
- Extended to 45 days from delivery
- Applies to all orders placed during this period
- Automatic - no special code required

**International Orders:**
- Same 30-day window (45 days during holidays)
- Customer responsible for return shipping costs
- Must clear customs - see Shipping Policy for documentation requirements
- Refunds processed after customs clearance confirmed

## Product Condition Guidelines

### Unopened Products
- **Action:** Full refund to original payment method
- **Timeline:** 3-5 business days after receipt
- **Approval:** Auto-approved for orders under $50

### Opened/Used Products
- **No refund:** Cash or original payment method
- **Store credit only:** 100% of purchase price
- **Rationale:** Prevents resale of partially used products while maintaining customer relationship
- **Exception:** If product caused adverse reaction (see Adverse Reactions section)

### Damaged/Defective Products
- **Action:** Full refund OR replacement (customer choice)
- **Requirements:** Photo documentation required
- **Additional:** 10% discount code for next purchase
- **Timeline:** Replacement shipped within 24 hours if in stock
- **Process:** See Damaged Shipment SOP for detailed procedures

## Refund Amount Escalation Thresholds

**Under $50:**
- Auto-approve refunds
- No manager approval needed
- Standard processing (3-5 days)

**$50 - $200:**
- Manager approval required
- Review customer history before approving
- Response within 4 hours during business hours

**Over $200:**
- Director approval required
- Full review: order history, return history, potential fraud indicators
- May require additional documentation
- Response within 24 hours

## Customer Tier Considerations

### VIP Customers (5+ orders in last 12 months)
- **Policy:** Apply generous interpretation of guidelines
- **Opened products:** Offer full refund (not just store credit) at manager discretion
- **Extended window:** Can approve returns up to 45 days even outside holiday period
- **Rationale:** LTV justifies flexibility - VIP customers average 3x lifetime value

### New Customers (First order)
- **Policy:** Standard guidelines apply strictly
- **Fraud prevention:** Photo documentation required for all damage claims
- **Note:** First-time returns establish pattern for future orders

### High Return Rate Customers (>30% return rate)
- **Policy:** Flag for review before processing
- **Action:** Standard refund approved, but account flagged
- **Follow-up:** Customer success team reaches out to understand issues
- **Escalation:** If return rate continues, may restrict to store credit only

## Adverse Reactions & Health Concerns

**If customer reports skin reaction or allergic response:**
- Immediate full refund (even if opened/used)
- No photo documentation required
- Expedite processing (24-48 hours)
- Flag product for quality review
- Document: product name, batch number, reaction type
- **Note:** This overrides all other restrictions

## Subscription Orders

**Active subscriptions:**
- Same return policy as one-time orders
- Returned items don't affect subscription status
- **Before processing return:** Offer to pause subscription instead of cancel
- See Subscription Cancellation SOP for pause procedures

**Cancelled subscriptions:**
- Can still return products ordered while subscription was active
- Standard 30-day window applies from original delivery date

## Special Cases

### Discontinued Products
- **Cannot offer replacement**
- Refund only option
- If customer wants to keep: offer 20% store credit compensation
- Check inventory before promising replacement on any return

### Gift Orders
- Recipient can return for store credit without proof of purchase
- Email gift giver for refund approval if refund requested
- Standard window applies from delivery date to recipient

### Sets/Bundles
- Must return entire set for full refund
- Partial returns: only accept if individual items sold separately
- Refund value: bundle price (not sum of individual prices)

## Fraud Prevention

**Photo requirement for damage claims:**
- Reduces fraudulent damage claims by ~60%
- Required for: damaged product, missing items, wrong product shipped
- Photos must show: product, packaging, shipping label
- **Waive requirement for:** VIP customers with no prior claims

**Multiple returns red flags:**
- More than 3 returns in 6 months from same customer
- Same product returned repeatedly (possible serial returner)
- High-value items with damage claims
- Action: Manual review before approval

## Processing Notes

**Inventory impact:**
- Unopened returns: can restock immediately
- Opened returns: cannot resell - write off as cost
- Damaged returns: flag carrier if pattern emerges (>3 per month)

**Cost thresholds:**
- Returns cost ~15% of order value on average (shipping + processing)
- For high-margin products (>50%), prefer replacement over refund when possible
- Track monthly return rate by product - target <8% overall
