# Shipping Policy
**Internal Operations Guide**
*Last Updated: April 2026*

## Overview
This document covers shipping procedures, timelines, and handling requirements for operational decision-making. Balance speed commitments with cost management and customer satisfaction.

## Domestic Shipping (US)

### Standard Shipping
- **Timeline:** 5-7 business days
- **Cost:** Free on orders $50+, otherwise $5.95
- **Carrier:** USPS or UPS (system selects based on destination/weight)
- **Tracking:** Provided within 24 hours of order placement

### Expedited Shipping
- **Timeline:** 2-3 business days
- **Cost:** $12.95 flat rate
- **Carrier:** UPS
- **Cutoff:** Orders placed before 2 PM ET ship same day
- **Note:** Weekends and holidays extend timeline

### Express Shipping
- **Timeline:** 1-2 business days (overnight for orders before 2 PM ET)
- **Cost:** $24.95 flat rate
- **Carrier:** UPS Express
- **Cutoff:** 2 PM ET for next-day delivery
- **Limitation:** Not available to PO boxes or APO/FPO addresses

### Order Processing Time
- **Standard:** Orders ship within 24 hours (business days)
- **High volume periods:** May extend to 48 hours (notify customer if delayed)
- **Out of stock items:** Customer contacted within 12 hours with options:
  - Wait for restock (ETA provided)
  - Substitute similar product
  - Partial refund for out-of-stock item

## International Shipping

### Available Countries
Currently ship to: Canada, UK, Australia, and EU member states

### International Timelines
- **Canada:** 7-10 business days
- **UK/EU:** 10-15 business days
- **Australia:** 12-18 business days

### International Costs
- **Canada:** $15.95 flat rate
- **UK/EU:** $24.95 flat rate
- **Australia:** $29.95 flat rate
- **Free shipping threshold:** $100+ (all international)

### Customs & Duties

**Customer Responsibility:**
- All customs duties, taxes, and import fees are customer's responsibility
- We cannot predict these charges - varies by country
- Clearly stated at checkout

**Customs Documentation:**
- We provide complete customs declaration on all international shipments
- Accurate product descriptions and values listed
- Required for customs clearance

**Customs Holds/Rejections:**
- If package held: Customer must contact local customs office
- If refused by customer: Return shipping cost deducted from refund
- If customs rejects: Customer responsible for return shipping or abandonment fees

**For International Returns:**
- Customer pays return shipping
- Package must clear US customs before refund processed
- Can take 3-6 weeks total
- Alternative: Offer store credit immediately to avoid customs delay

## Address Verification

### Invalid/Incomplete Addresses
**System flags if:**
- Address doesn't validate with carrier
- Missing apartment/unit number
- PO Box when express shipping selected (not allowed)

**Action:**
1. Contact customer immediately via email and phone
2. Hold order - do not ship to invalid address
3. Update address in system after confirmation
4. Ship within 24 hours of confirmation

**Cost:**
- If delay is our error: Upgrade to expedited at no charge
- If customer provided wrong address: Standard processing applies

### Address Change Requests

**Before shipment:**
- Can update address at no charge
- Confirm new address via email
- Document change in order notes

**After shipment:**
- **Cannot change destination** once carrier has package
- If customer wants to refuse delivery: Follow refused package procedure (see below)
- If reshipment needed: Customer pays shipping for second shipment

### Refused Packages
**If customer refuses delivery:**
- Package returns to us at no charge (carrier covers)
- Once received: Process refund minus original shipping cost
- If customer wants reshipment: Charge shipping for new delivery

## Shipping Issues

### Lost Packages

**Timeline triggers:**
- Domestic: Not delivered 10 days after expected date
- International: Not delivered 25 days after expected date

**Process:**
1. Check tracking - verify actually lost (not just delayed)
2. File carrier claim (we handle this)
3. **Immediate action for customer:**
   - Reship replacement immediately (don't wait for claim resolution)
   - Or offer full refund (customer choice)
4. Document: Order number, tracking number, date reported

**Cost:**
- No charge to customer
- Insurance covers our cost
- Priority flag: Ship replacement via expedited at no charge

### Delayed Packages

**Expected delays:**
- Weather events: 2-5 day extension normal
- Holiday periods: Expect 3-7 day delays (notify customers proactively)
- Carrier delays: Check carrier service alerts

**Action:**
1. Check carrier tracking for updates
2. If domestic order > 10 days: Contact carrier
3. If international > expected timeline by 7 days: Contact carrier
4. Provide customer with update and revised ETA

**Customer compensation:**
- If domestic order > 15 days: $10 store credit
- If domestic order > 20 days: $20 store credit + consider reship
- International delays: No automatic compensation (expected variability)

### Damaged in Transit

See **Damaged Shipment SOP** for complete procedures.

**Quick reference:**
- Customer must provide photos
- Reship or refund (customer choice)
- 10% discount code for inconvenience
- Flag carrier if >3 damages per month
- VIP customers: Photo requirement waived

## PO Boxes & Military Addresses

### PO Boxes
- **Allowed:** Standard and Expedited shipping only
- **Not allowed:** Express shipping (requires signature)
- System should prevent selection at checkout

### APO/FPO/DPO (Military)
- **Timeline:** 10-20 business days (variable)
- **Cost:** Treated as domestic rate
- **Carrier:** USPS only option
- **Customs:** No customs fees (US military mail)
- **Note:** Cannot guarantee delivery timeframes due to military routing

## Subscription Orders

### Recurring Shipments
- **Default:** Standard shipping (free for orders $50+)
- **Can select:** Expedited at recurring charge ($12.95/shipment)
- **Processing:** Ships on same day each cycle
- **Address updates:** Must update before next cycle process date

### Subscription Shipping Issues
- If shipment fails: Automatically reschedule next attempt in 7 days
- Customer notified of failure reason (payment, address, out of stock)
- See Subscription Cancellation SOP for pause/cancel procedures

## Inventory Impact on Shipping

### Stock Check Before Promise
**Before promising shipping timeline:**
1. Verify all items in stock
2. If low stock (<10 units): Hold order for QA check (might be damaged inventory)
3. If out of stock: See out-of-stock procedure above

### Partial Shipments
**Policy:** We don't do partial shipments by default

**Exception:**
- If high-value item in stock, low-value item out of stock
- AND customer is VIP (5+ orders)
- Can ship available items immediately, backorder rest at no additional shipping cost
- **Requires manager approval**

## Shipping & Returns Cross-Reference

### Return Shipments (Customer to Us)
- Customer pays return shipping (unless error on our part)
- We provide return label upon request for $7.95 (deducted from refund)
- International returns: Customer arranges shipping, provides tracking
- See Return & Refund Policy for complete procedures

### Replacement Shipments (Us to Customer)
- **Damaged products:** We pay shipping (expedited at no charge)
- **Wrong item shipped:** We pay shipping (expedited at no charge)
- **Customer opened wrong item:** Customer pays return, we pay replacement shipping
- See Damaged Shipment SOP for detailed procedures

## Seasonal Considerations

### Peak Holiday Season (Nov 15 - Jan 5)
- **Volume:** 3-4x normal
- **Processing time:** May extend to 48-72 hours
- **Carrier delays:** Common - set expectations proactively
- **Cutoff dates:** Communicate clearly (e.g., "Order by Dec 18 for Christmas delivery")
- **Staff accordingly:** Double ops team during this period

### Summer (June - August)
- **Heat concerns:** Formulations stable, but avoid leaving in hot mailboxes
- **Sunscreen demand:** Monitor stock levels - Mineral and Tinted SPF high volume
- **Vacation holds:** More address change requests (travelers)

### Extreme Weather
- **Natural disasters:** May suspend shipping to affected areas
- **Policy:** Safety first - delay deliveries rather than risk driver safety
- **Communication:** Proactive email to customers in affected regions

## Carrier Performance Tracking

### Monthly Metrics to Monitor
- Delivery success rate (target: >98%)
- Average delivery time vs promised
- Damage rate per carrier (target: <0.5%)
- Lost package rate (target: <0.2%)

### When to Switch Carriers
- If carrier consistently fails metrics for 2+ months
- Multiple damage incidents (>3/month)
- Excessive delays without resolution

**Escalation:** Operations Manager reviews carrier performance quarterly

## Cost Management

### Shipping Cost Structure
- Standard domestic: $3.50 actual cost (we charge $5.95 or free)
- Expedited: $8.50 actual cost (we charge $12.95)
- Express: $18.00 actual cost (we charge $24.95)
- Margin covers packaging materials and labor

### When to Offer Free Shipping Upgrades
- Our shipping error (wrong address attempt, delayed processing)
- VIP customers with complaint
- Order value >$200
- Compensation for damaged shipment reships

### When NOT to Offer Free Upgrades
- Customer address error
- Carrier delays beyond our control (unless >15 days)
- Customer request without justification
